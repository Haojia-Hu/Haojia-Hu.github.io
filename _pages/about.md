Haojia Hu is a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at American University in Washington, DC.

His research interests are in macroeconomics and finance, with a focus on housing market,  household behavior, inflation, and monetary policy. Methodologically, his research combines applied quantative analysis with high-frequency data and dynamic models to understand macroeconomic adjustment.
